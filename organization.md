# About our organization

We are a start-up security company with focus on pentests. Our specialisations are internal enterprise networks (Active Directory and Entra ID infrastructures) and web applications.

With our clear focus, we have the greatest possible know-how in the field of offensive IT security.

We are also developing innovative projects that take our pentests to the next level.
